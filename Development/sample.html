Hogwarts Legacy

Hogwarts Legacy is an immersive, open-world action role-playing game (RPG) set in the 1800s Wizarding World, a century before the events of the Harry Potter books and films. Players create their own witch or wizard character and attend Hogwarts, where they learn spells, brew potions, and discover a unique, original storyline.
